# Agent Attachment Map

This map is intentionally explicit. It exists so the operator, the maintainer, and the future validation workflow can see exactly which shared knowledge files feed the prime-agent attachment set and why they matter.

1. Knowledge - 01 - Overview and About
- Purpose: define what DCOIR is, what the collector line is, what the Gemini bundle is, and what the workflow is trying to achieve.

2. Knowledge - 02 - Elastic Quick Start
- Purpose: preserve command-lane, response-action, and telemetry-query startup guidance.

3. Knowledge - 03 - Local Test and Regression
- Purpose: preserve local versus response-action lane distinctions and test expectations.

4. Knowledge - 04 - Tier 1 Collect Runbook
- Purpose: preserve baseline and targeted collection initiation guidance.

5. Knowledge - 05 - Tier 2 Collect Runbook
- Purpose: preserve deeper-collection logic and escalation context.

6. Knowledge - 06 - Enrichment Actions
- Purpose: preserve enrich-session behavior, action selection, finalize behavior, and retrieval expectations.

7. Knowledge - 07 - Artifact Review Guide
- Purpose: preserve collector-artifact interpretation, upload-order guidance, and retrieved-artifact review logic.

8. Knowledge - 08 - Troubleshooting
- Purpose: preserve bounded troubleshooting rules and non-overreach behavior.

9. Knowledge - 09 - FAQ
- Purpose: preserve operator-facing answers for repeated workflow questions.

10. Knowledge - 10 - AI Prompt and Agent Design
- Purpose: preserve the major-version prompt and agent design posture, including verbosity and explicitness requirements.

11. Knowledge - 11 - IOC Enrichment and Public Sources
- Purpose: preserve mixed-format IOC parsing, IOC typing, enrichment boundaries, and public-source corroboration rules.
