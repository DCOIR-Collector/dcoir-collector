DCOIR Viable Deliverable Generation Contract And v2 Bundle Method - 2026-04-06

Current state id: 2026-04-06-viable-deliverable-generation-contract-durable-v1

Purpose
- Record the durable method that produced the first operator-acceptable v2 Gemini bundle layout and v2 standalone prompt bundle layout.
- Preserve that method in governed state so future sessions do not regress into shipping stored generated artifacts, thin Gemini files, or condensed standalone prompts.

What was learned
- The old DCOIR Gemini email build bundle remains useful only for structure and naming lessons.
- The original alert-triage Gemini agents remain the correct density and field-writing benchmark because their Description and Instructions fields are routing-rich, explicit, and operationally detailed.
- Gemini deliverables must be generated fresh every round and must not default to shipping stored generated markdowns from the repo.
- The standalone prompt deliverable must be a true full assembly of PP-01 through PP-07 rather than a condensed rewrite that pushes missing content into attachments.
- Operator-facing test bundles must stay minimal, structurally clear, and free of unrelated repo materials.

Accepted v2 bundle method
- Gemini bundle shape:
  - `00_START_HERE/`
  - `01_GEMINI_AGENT_BUILD/`
  - `02_PRIME_AGENT_ATTACHMENTS/`
- Standalone bundle shape:
  - `00_START_HERE/`
  - `01_STANDALONE_PROMPT/`
  - `02_PROMPT_ATTACHMENTS/`
- Gemini content method:
  - use the old alert-triage agent set as the verbosity and field-writing benchmark
  - generate a fresh parent and seven fresh sub-agents
  - keep only operator-facing build files and required attachment files in the shipment
- Standalone content method:
  - assemble the all-in-one prompt directly from PP-01 through PP-07
  - use attachments only as supporting runtime knowledge, not as substitutes for omitted prompt-pack substance

Durable rules promoted now
- Added DOC-04 as the durable deliverable-generation contract.
- Hardened PP-09 so future Gemini bundle work must use the approved hierarchy, fresh-generation-only posture, thin-output rejection gates, and accepted bundle layout.
- Refreshed the continuity surfaces so the project now names the viable generation method explicitly instead of burying it in chat history.

What this does not yet claim
- It does not claim the v2 Gemini or standalone content is final.
- It does not claim live-use wording refinement is complete.
- It does claim that the v2 generation method is the current viable baseline for future iterative refreshes.

Best next move
- Continue live testing the v2 Gemini bundle and v2 standalone prompt bundle, capture concrete operator feedback, and refine DOC-04 plus PP-09 further if later rounds show another repeatable improvement that should become durable.
