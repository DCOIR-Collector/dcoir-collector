What changed
- Added `project_sources/LOG-25_DCOIR_Stateful_Local_JSON_Startup_Preflight_Rollout_2026-04-04.txt` as the governed decision record for the bounded rollout of beginning-of-session local-JSON presence preflights across the confirmed in-scope DCOIR skills.

- The rollout needed to stay bounded to the skills that really use local JSON working-state files instead of touching unrelated stateful skills.

- Updated `dcoir-session-tracker` and `dcoir-plan-tracker` skill packages passed local packaging validation.
- The next immediate branch is a live workflow validation of the startup local-JSON preflight behavior on a real governed push or local plan-tracker branch.
