# Gemini Field Mapping and Routing Guidance

## Purpose
Keep routing language, description density, and escalation triggers explicit enough to preserve the intended triage-to-DCOIR handoff.

## Core guidance
- description is routing-critical and should absorb the strongest useful wording from purpose, role, description, and duplicated scope text when that improves routing accuracy
- make escalation triggers operationally explicit
- preserve tool-usage expectations, output contract, and stop conditions in the routed unit
- distinguish authoritative prompt-pack sources from comparative references and generated companions

## Handoff fields to watch
- trigger condition
- summary of why escalation is needed
- exact next DCOIR step
- evidence or artifact expected next
- bounded-confidence or stop conditions
